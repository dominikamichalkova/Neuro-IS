Neuro-IS team at University of Strathclyde Glasgow

NEURO-IS is a research group within The Data Analytics, Software Systems, and Interaction Group of the Department of Computer & Information Sciences at University of Strathclyde Glasgow working in the area of information retrieval using advanced techniques of capturing brain signals.
